Procedures & Policies

Our Training & Placement Cell follows structured procedures to ensure smooth & transparent placement activities. For detailed policies & regulations, please contact us directly.

Step 1: Registration

Students register for the placement drive through the online portal.

2: Pre-Placement Talk

Companies conduct a pre-placement session to discuss roles and expectations.

3: Eligibility Check

The team verifies eligibility based on academic & other criteria.

4: Resume Submission

Students submit their resumes for evaluation & shortlisting.

5: Selection Process

Candidates go through tests, interviews, & group discussions as required.

6: Feedback & Evaluation

Candidates receive feedback on their performance from the recruiters.

7: Final Selection

Recruiters finalize the list of selected candidates for job offers.

8: Offer & Acceptance

Successful candidates receive job offers, which they can accept or decline.

FAQs

How do I register for recruitment activities?

You can register by contacting the T&P Cell directly or via our online registration form available on the GGV website.

What facilities are provided during campus visits?

We provide state-of-the-art infrastructure, internet facilities, & assistance for smooth recruitment processes.

Can accommodations be arranged for recruiters?

Yes, we offer on-campus lodging options or recommend nearby hotels for comfortable accommodations.
