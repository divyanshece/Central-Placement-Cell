Overview

The Training & Placement Cell, School of Studies of Engineering & Technology (T&P Cell, SOSET) of Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur, is dedicated to facilitating student placements across various disciplines of Engineering. The T&P Cell, SOSET collaborates with leading organizations to provide internship & training opportunities, ensuring students are career-ready.

The placement season runs from late July to March, during which numerous companies conduct campus interviews. The T&P Cell, SOSET is equipped with modern facilities for group discussions & interviews, & it organizes pre-placement talks to foster interaction between students & potential employers.

A committee of student representatives from undergraduate programs supports the T&P Cell, SOSET in developing annual policies & guidelines for the placement process. This collaboration ensures that student feedback is integral to decision-making.
